It All Started Up One Weekend ....

The UserBallot team met at the first ever Tri-Valley Startup Weekend in September 2013. We saw a need for companies to gather feedback from users in the most lightweight mannner possible. Just two days later, UserBallot was born.

Our guiding principles are:

  • Users shouldn't have to work at giving you feedback.
  • Developers shouldn't need to be social or mass media experts
  • Business owners shouldn't need an advanced degree in informatics to know what their users want
  • Any product you need to add to your own application should be, well, worth adding to your application
