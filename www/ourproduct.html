Our Product

UserBallot is a new one-touch, yes/no feedback gathering tool that lets website owners get real-time answers from more of your customers than ever before, and all you need to do is copy and paste a few lines of code into your website!

By making simple questions that only require one touch to answer, you will have a larger portion of your audience giving you feedback, allowing you to make better decisions about what you care about.

We start you off with some sample questions so you don’t get stumped, but you can ask your users anything you’d like!

Beyond the questions, our built in analytics give you powerful insight into what your audience is into!


Best of all, while it’s in beta, UserBallot is free! Give us a try today!
